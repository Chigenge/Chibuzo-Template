About Us

The Ado Business Incubation Centre is a one-stop centre for business owners, startup founders, funders, and everyone in between providing support to early-stage and existing businesses proferring innovative solutions to economic, environmental, and social challenges.

Our Core Offerings

Our Programme makes provision to help accelerate early-stage startups that are offering innovative solutions to economic, environmental, and social challenges specifically across our focus areas. These provisions include:

  • Business Incubation
  • Digital Skills Development
  • Innovative Management
  • Mentorship and Entrepreneurship Support
  • Finance and Investor Support
  • Business Advisory
